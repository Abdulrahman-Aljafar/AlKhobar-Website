Tourism

Al Khobar is home to a vast sea, beautiful beaches and excellent fishing spots. It is a favorite destination for tourists from different parts of the Kingdom. There is King Fahd tourist city, which includes parks, fun and games halls. As well as waterfront and other tourist facilities and There are a number of new parks, including children's games, football fields, basketball, concrete walkers, green spaces, trees, shrubs, flowers, lighting networks, irrigation systems and pumps.
